# Sample Job Description for Testing

## Company: TechCorp Solutions
## Role: Software Development Engineer

### About the Role
We are looking for a talented Software Development Engineer to join our growing team. You will work on building scalable web applications and contribute to our cloud infrastructure.

### Required Skills
- Strong proficiency in **Java** and **Python**
- Experience with **React** and **Node.js** for full-stack development
- Solid understanding of **Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA)**
- Knowledge of **Object-Oriented Programming (OOP)** principles
- Experience with **SQL** databases (MySQL or PostgreSQL)
- Familiarity with **REST APIs** and **GraphQL**
- Understanding of **DBMS** concepts including normalization and indexing
- Knowledge of **Operating Systems** and **Computer Networks**

### Nice to Have
- Experience with **AWS** or other cloud platforms
- Knowledge of **Docker** and **Kubernetes**
- Experience with **CI/CD** pipelines
- Familiarity with **MongoDB** or other NoSQL databases
- Experience with testing frameworks like **JUnit** or **PyTest**

### Responsibilities
- Design and develop scalable web applications
- Write clean, maintainable code following best practices
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams
- Participate in code reviews and technical discussions
- Optimize application performance and troubleshoot issues

### Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
- 0-2 years of experience (freshers welcome)
- Strong problem-solving skills
- Good communication and teamwork abilities
